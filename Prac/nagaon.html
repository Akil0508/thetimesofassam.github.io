About NAGAON

Nagaon, is a city and a municipal board in Nagaon district in the Indian state of Assam. It is situated 121 kilometres east of Guwahati. It has a population of 147,231. In education section, total literates in Nagaon city are 98,068 of which 52,690 are males while 45,378 are females.
