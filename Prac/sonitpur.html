About SONITPUR

Sonitpur district was created in 1983 when it was split from Darrang.
Udalguri was also carved out for the Bodoland Territorial Area Districts.
Sonitpur district has a long history starting with Kamrupa Mlechha rule, which divided into Baro-Bhuyan rule and Chutiya kingdom rule.
The border between the two kingdoms was the Kameng/Jia Bhorali River.
Later, the Ahoms occupied the region during the period 1505-10 AD under the rule of Dihingia raja.
